Key Information


Why TB Detection

Tuberculosis is a serious infectious disease that can have severe health consequences if not detected and treated early. Early detection through effective screening methods is crucial for controlling the spread of TB.


Disease Symptoms

  • Persistent cough
  • Coughing up blood
  • Fever
  • Night sweats
  • Loss of appetite
  • Weight loss
  • Fatigue


Preventive Measures

  • Good ventilation
  • Avoiding close contact with infected individuals
  • Early detection and treatment
  • Proper hygiene practices
  • Use of masks in crowded areas
